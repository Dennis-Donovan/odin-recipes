A nice smoothie recipe (I think it's nice anyways)

This is my simple recipe for a slightly modified strawberry banana smoothie. Instead of slices of strawberry
it uses strawberry puree and it also includes frozen blueberries and frozen raspberries

Ingredients:

  • 1 banana
  • 5.3 oz plain or vanilla yogurt
  • 4 oz strawberry puree
  • 4 oz whole milk
  • 1/3 cup frozen blueberries
  • 1/3 cup frozen raspberries

Steps:

  1. Add all ingredients to blender
  2. Blend
  3. Pour into a big cup
  4. Take a nice sip
  5. Mmmm.
  6. Okay clean blender before it's too late
  7. Enjoy nice smoothie
